5 Optical Illusions That Will Blow Your Mind


Watch these 5 illusion videos. They start easy and get more confusing. Can you understand them?


5. Which way is she spinning?

The Spinning Dancer illusion shows a picture of a dancer spinning. But which way is she spinning? Some people say clockwise, and others say counterclockwise. Many people say the dancer changes direction. This happens because the brain gets confused. Without a clear reference point, the brain is tricked by the dancer's shape and how she turns.



4. Moving mask

People with schizophrenia can see this illusion right away and are not tricked by it. A study by students in Germany and
the UK found that people with schizophrenia are better at separating "what their eyes see and what their brain thinks it sees."



3. How many black dots can you see?

The answer is zero. Even if your eyes say something different, there are no black dots. There are only white
dots that look like they get darker at the edges of your vision. This is called the scintillating grid illusion.
Scientists have different ideas about why this happens, but no one really knows for sure. Its just that our brains can trick us sometimes.



2. Which circle is bigger?

Yep, your brain sucks again. The orange circles are exactly the same. This is called the Ebbinghaus illusion.
Experts think that the difference in distance between the outer ring and the inner circles, as well as how the ring around the
"smaller" circle looks, causes this illusion. Heres an animation that shows the illusion in real time.
